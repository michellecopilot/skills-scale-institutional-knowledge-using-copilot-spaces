# OctoAcme — Role-Specific Onboarding Checklist

## Purpose
Support consistent and efficient onboarding of new team members by clarifying role expectations, key responsibilities, and interaction points.

---

## For All Roles

- [ ] Read [Project Management Overview](./octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Review [Roles & Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) to understand your role and how you interact with others
- [ ] Join relevant Slack channels and distribution lists
- [ ] Add recurring meetings to calendar (standups, syncs, retros)
- [ ] Familiarize yourself with team project board (GitHub Projects or equivalent)
- [ ] Request access to key tools (GitHub, CI/CD, monitoring, docs)
- [ ] Shadow one project cycle before leading/owning activities

---

## For Developers

### Week 1: Foundations
- [ ] Clone repository and set up local dev environment
- [ ] Run existing test suite and verify it passes
- [ ] Read [Execution & Tracking](./octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md) — focus on PR workflow and Definition of Done
- [ ] Review the project's coding standards and conventions
- [ ] Set up IDE with required linting and formatting tools
- [ ] Pair with experienced developer on first small task

### Week 2-3: Contribution
- [ ] Make first contribution: small bug fix or documentation update
- [ ] Participate in code review process (both as reviewer and author)
- [ ] Attend sprint planning and understand current backlog priorities
- [ ] Review test coverage expectations and write tests for new code

### Ongoing
- [ ] Understand CI/CD pipeline and how to troubleshoot failed builds
- [ ] Participate in design reviews with UX Designer and Product Manager
- [ ] Communicate blockers to Scrum Master immediately
- [ ] Join weekly delivery sync and share status updates

---

## For Product Managers

### Week 1: Strategy & Context
- [ ] Review company vision, strategy, and customer problem areas
- [ ] Read all process docs: [Overview](./octoacme-project-management-overview.md), [Planning](./octoacme-project-planning.md), [Execution & Tracking](./octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md)
- [ ] Understand current roadmap, active projects, and stakeholders
- [ ] Schedule 1-on-1s with Project Manager, Business Analyst, key Developers
- [ ] Review recent retrospectives and action items

### Week 2-3: Product Deep Dive
- [ ] Spend time with users or customers to understand problems
- [ ] Document initial product questions and improvement ideas
- [ ] Review metrics and KPIs tied to product success
- [ ] Meet with UX Designer to understand design direction and user research

### Ongoing
- [ ] Maintain prioritized backlog with clear acceptance criteria
- [ ] Define success metrics for new features during planning
- [ ] Participate in weekly syncs with Project Manager and leads
- [ ] Review and approve designs and feature implementations
- [ ] Measure outcomes against metrics; share learning with team

---

## For Project Managers

### Week 1: Process & Stakeholders
- [ ] Read all process docs, especially [Overview](./octoacme-project-management-overview.md) and [Planning](./octoacme-project-planning.md)
- [ ] Map stakeholders, communication needs, and escalation paths
- [ ] Review active projects, timelines, and risk registers
- [ ] Understand company/team constraints (budget, resources, dependencies)
- [ ] Schedule 1-on-1s with Product Manager, Scrum Master, key team leads

### Week 2-3: Execution
- [ ] Observe or facilitate a project planning session
- [ ] Learn the project board setup and how to track progress
- [ ] Review risk management and escalation procedures
- [ ] Attend standup, delivery sync, and retrospective

### Ongoing
- [ ] Maintain project plans and timelines with team input
- [ ] Proactively identify and communicate risks
- [ ] Facilitate meetings and remove blockers
- [ ] Provide weekly status updates to stakeholders
- [ ] Measure on-time and on-scope delivery; share metrics with leadership

---

## For Scrum Masters (if applicable)

### Week 1: Agile Principles & Team
- [ ] Read [Execution & Tracking](./octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md) and understand team rhythm
- [ ] Review [Roles & Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) — especially Scrum Master section
- [ ] Shadow or co-facilitate standup, planning, and retrospective with experienced Scrum Master
- [ ] Learn team's current velocity, sprint length, and ceremony schedule
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand project context and constraints

### Week 2-3: Facilitation
- [ ] Take primary role in one standup and retrospective
- [ ] Help team establish or refine Definition of Done
- [ ] Work with team on identifying and removing impediments
- [ ] Review sprint metrics (velocity, burn-down) and identify improvement areas

### Ongoing
- [ ] Facilitate all Agile ceremonies consistently
- [ ] Coach team on Agile best practices and continuous improvement
- [ ] Track and share team health metrics
- [ ] Escalate systemic blockers to Project Manager and Product Manager
- [ ] Measure team satisfaction and team velocity; identify trends

---

## For UX Designers

### Week 1: Product & Users
- [ ] Review [Project Management Overview](./octoacme-project-management-overview.md) to understand product lifecycle
- [ ] Review [Roles & Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) — especially UX Designer section
- [ ] Spend time with Product Manager understanding user research, personas, and design direction
- [ ] Review existing product, design system, and component library
- [ ] Understand key user flows and current pain points

### Week 2-3: Collaboration
- [ ] Participate in design review with Product Manager and Developers
- [ ] Create or contribute to wireframes/prototypes for in-flight features
- [ ] Document design specifications for handoff to developers
- [ ] Attend sprint planning to understand upcoming features

### Ongoing
- [ ] Conduct or contribute to user research and usability testing
- [ ] Maintain design system and component documentation
- [ ] Collaborate with Developers during implementation (reviews, feedback)
- [ ] Iterate based on user feedback and QA findings
- [ ] Share design learnings in team meetings and retros

---

## For DevOps Engineers

### Week 1: Infrastructure & Pipelines
- [ ] Read [Execution & Tracking](./octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md) and [Release & Deployment](./octoacme-release-and-deployment.md)
- [ ] Review [Roles & Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) — especially DevOps Engineer section
- [ ] Understand current infrastructure, CI/CD pipeline, and deployment procedures
- [ ] Review monitoring, alerting, and incident response procedures
- [ ] Meet with Developers and on-call support to understand pain points

### Week 2-3: Hands-On
- [ ] Trigger a test deployment and walk through the entire process
- [ ] Review CI/CD logs and understand how to troubleshoot failures
- [ ] Work with Developers to optimize build times or pipeline efficiency
- [ ] Document infrastructure setup in a runbook for consistency

### Ongoing
- [ ] Support Developers with environment and pipeline issues
- [ ] Execute releases coordinating with Project Manager and QA
- [ ] Monitor system performance and reliability
- [ ] Collaborate on infrastructure improvements and scaling strategies
- [ ] Share reliability metrics and incident learnings with team

---

## For Business Analysts

### Week 1: Strategy & Processes
- [ ] Read all process docs, especially [Planning](./octoacme-project-planning.md) and [Project Initiation](./octoacme-project-initiation.md)
- [ ] Review [Roles & Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) — especially Business Analyst section
- [ ] Understand business strategy, customer problems, and success metrics
- [ ] Review existing requirements documentation and templates
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager and Project Manager to understand current priorities

### Week 2-3: Documentation
- [ ] Contribute to requirements documentation for one upcoming feature
- [ ] Learn traceability practices and review existing matrices
- [ ] Participate in requirements review with Developers and QA
- [ ] Understand acceptance criteria definition and validation

### Ongoing
- [ ] Gather requirements from stakeholders and Product Manager
- [ ] Create clear, testable specifications with acceptance criteria
- [ ] Establish traceability between requirements, design, and test cases
- [ ] Support QA in validating implementations against requirements
- [ ] Measure and report on success metrics post-delivery
- [ ] Document lessons learned for future projects

---

## Onboarding Sign-Off

Once the new team member has completed all applicable checklist items:

- [ ] Manager confirms all items complete
- [ ] New team member confirms understanding of role and interactions
- [ ] Schedule follow-up 1-on-1 at 30-day and 90-day marks to assess progress
- [ ] Add to team Slack/distribution lists for ongoing communication
- [ ] Capture any onboarding feedback to improve this checklist
